Click any marker — Fremantle, Alfama, Shimokitazawa, and nine others are seeded for this prototype. Each generates a short character note: what the place feels like, who it suits, a few anchors, and one thing that isn't obvious from a map.

Prototype scope: 12 hand-picked neighbourhoods across Perth, Lisbon, and Tokyo, so you can judge whether the generated notes are actually useful before this goes any wider. A working version would let you click anywhere — not just seeded pins — and would ground each note in real source text (Wikivoyage, OSM tags) rather than the model's own knowledge alone.
